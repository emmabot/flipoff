Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

The Short Version: Lil Sauce does not sell your data. Period. We collect only what's needed to make the app work for you.

What We Collect

Free Version

Nothing. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics.

Your mode preference and sound setting are stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

The app fetches content from our server and weather data from the Open-Meteo API. Neither request includes any personal information.

Premium Version (when you upgrade)

When you unlock premium features, we collect:

  • Email address — to create your account and send important updates about the service.
  • Display name — shown on your board and visible to guests who visit your board link.
  • Calendar data — event titles, times, and details from calendars you choose to connect. We fetch this to display your schedule on the board.
  • Location (zip code) — to show local weather. We convert this to coordinates and send only coordinates to our weather provider. We never send your zip code or address to any third party.
  • Custom messages — text you create to display on your board.
  • Guest messages — text that guests send to your board via shared links.
  • Board preferences — your settings, connected services, and board configuration.

How We Use Your Data

  • Calendar data is displayed on your board and refreshed periodically. It is not used for anything else.
  • Email is used for account management and important service updates only. We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in.
  • Location is used for weather lookups only.
  • Messages (custom and guest) are displayed on your board only.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

Where Your Data Lives

  • Stored in Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure).
  • Secured with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Calendar tokens are stored securely and refreshed automatically.

Third-Party Services

Lil Sauce uses a small number of third-party services, each for a specific purpose:

  • Firebase (Google) — data storage and real-time sync for premium features.
  • Google Calendar API — calendar integration, only with your explicit permission.
  • Open-Meteo — weather data. Receives only geographic coordinates, no personal information.

We do not use any advertising SDKs or analytics trackers.

Guest Send

Premium users can share a board link so friends and family can send messages to their TV.

  • Guest messages are not tied to any account — they are anonymous.
  • Messages auto-expire and are deleted after 24 hours.
  • You can disable Guest Send at any time from your board settings.

Children's Privacy

Lil Sauce Kids mode is designed for children ages 4 and up.

  • The free version collects no data from any user, including children.
  • Premium features require a parent or guardian account.
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent.

Your Rights

  • Delete your account — contact us and we'll delete all your data within 30 days.
  • Export your data — request a copy of everything we have about you.
  • Revoke calendar access — disconnect Google Calendar at any time from your board settings.
  • Disable Guest Send — turn it off in your board settings whenever you like.

Data Retention

  • Account data — kept until you delete your account.
  • Guest messages — automatically deleted after 24 hours.
  • Calendar data — cached locally on your device, refreshed from source, and not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Custom messages — kept until you delete them or your account.

Changes to This Policy

We'll update this page when things change. For major changes, we'll notify premium users via email and all users via an in-app notice.
