Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

The Short Version: Lil Sauce does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

What We Collect

Nothing. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics.

Your mode preference and sound setting are stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

The app fetches content from our server and weather data from the Open-Meteo API. Neither request includes any personal information.

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with advertisers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

Lil Sauce uses one third-party service:

  • Open-Meteo — weather data. Receives only geographic coordinates, no personal information.

We do not use any advertising SDKs or analytics trackers.

Children's Privacy

Lil Sauce Kids mode is designed for children ages 4 and up.

  • The app collects no data from any user, including children.
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is nothing to delete or export. If you have questions, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We'll update this page when things change. Major changes will be communicated via an in-app notice.
