Lil Sauce Support

A little something for your screen.

Lil Sauce is a split-flap display app for Apple TV that brings fun, inspiration, and useful info to your biggest screen. Choose from three content modes — Kids, Innovator, or History — and let the board do its thing.

Need help? Email us at support@lilsauce.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change modes?

Press the Menu button on your Siri Remote to open the settings overlay. From there, select a different mode (Kids, Innovator, or History). Your choice is saved automatically.

How do I toggle sound on or off?

Press the Menu button on your Siri Remote to open the settings overlay. You'll see a sound toggle — tap it to turn the split-flap sound effect on or off. Your preference is saved automatically.

How do I navigate with the Siri Remote?

Use the touch surface to swipe and highlight options. Press the center (select) button to confirm. Press Menu to open or close settings. The app is designed to work entirely with the Siri Remote — no additional controllers needed.

Does Lil Sauce collect any data?

No. The free version collects zero data. Your mode and sound preferences are stored locally on your Apple TV and never leave your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What content is in each mode?

Kids — jokes, riddles, fun facts, vocabulary, math puzzles, morning reminders with weather, and bedtime quotes. Innovator — tech history, inventor quotes, science breakthroughs, and startup wisdom. History — world history events, famous quotes, civilization milestones, and geography facts.

Is Lil Sauce safe for children?

Yes. Kids mode is designed for ages 4 and up. All content is family-friendly and age-appropriate. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

The app shows a blank screen. What do I do?

Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. On Apple TV, double-press the TV button on the Siri Remote, swipe up on Lil Sauce to close it, then relaunch. If the issue persists, email us at support@lilsauce.com.
