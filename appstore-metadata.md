## Description
Turn your Apple TV into a beautiful split-flap display — like the ones you see in airports and train stations.

Lil Sauce shows an ever-changing stream of messages on a realistic animated board. Choose from three curated content modes:

🎮 KIDS MODE
Jokes, riddles, knock-knocks, fun facts, math puzzles, vocabulary words, and games like Rock Paper Scissors and Color Mixing. Educational and fun for ages 4-10.

💡 INNOVATOR MODE
Tech history, inventor quotes, science breakthroughs, startup wisdom, and Women in STEM facts. Inspiring content for builders, makers, and the curious.

📜 HISTORY MODE
World events, famous historical quotes, civilization milestones, ancient wonders, and cultural traditions from around the globe.

Features:
• Realistic split-flap animation with mechanical click sounds
• Morning weather and reminders (Kids mode)
• Bedtime quotes and calming messages (Kids mode)
• Interactive games: Rock Paper Scissors, Unscramble, Math Puzzles
• 1,500+ curated messages across all modes
• Navigate with Siri Remote — swipe, click, and discover
• Sound toggle with Play/Pause button

Just pick a mode and let it run. Perfect as a family message board, conversation starter, or ambient display.
